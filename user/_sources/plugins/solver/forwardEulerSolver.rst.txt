===========================
 ForwardEulerSolver plugin
===========================

The ForwardEulerSolver plugin implements the forward `Euler method <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euler_method>`__ to solve `ODEs <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ordinary_differential_equation>`__ and it can be customised through the following property:

- **Step:** the step used by the solver (default: :math:`1`).
